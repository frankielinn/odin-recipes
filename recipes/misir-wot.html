How to Make Misir Wot

Let's make your new favorite red lentil dish!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter or ghee
  • 1 cup onion, chopped fine
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5-2 tablespoons berbere seasoning
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed 2-3 times
  • 2.5 cups water
  • lime juice, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat a heavy bottom sauce pot or dutch oven. Add butter or ghee, along with chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until onions soften.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger and sauté another minute.
  3. Add tomato paste, salt and Berbere seasoning / spice mix. Stir well. Add rinsed and drained lentils, 3 cups water and stir.
  4. Bring it to a boil, then reduce heat to medium, cover the pot and cook for 12-15 minutes, or until the lentils cook through and reach a creamy consistency. Be sure to stir every few minutes or so. If the stew seems too thick, add ½ - 1 cup water (or as needed) and stir well till the water gets absorbed and lentils reach a creamy consistency. Check for seasoning.
  5. Finish the dish with a light squeeze of lime juice. Serve with the traditional sourdough flatbread, injera. Enjoy!
